Eid ul-Adha — woh din jab Hazrat Ibrahim (AS) ne apni sab se qeemti cheez Allah ki raah mein pesh kar di. Unki is be-misaal qurbani ne yeh sikhaya ke sachcha iman har cheez se bada hota hai. Aaj hum usi rooh ko yaad karte hain — apne dil mein ikhlaas, apne ghar mein sukoon, aur apnon ke liye duaayein lekar.

Qurbani sirf janwar ki nahi hoti — apna ghuroor, apni nafrat, apni takabbur bhi Allah ki raah mein dedo. Yahi asli Eid hai.
